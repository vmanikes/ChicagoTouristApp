Welcome to the windy city

In 2014, Chicago had 50.2 million international and domestic visitors.Chicago's culture includes the visual arts, novels, film, theater, especially improvisational comedy, and music, particularly jazz, blues, soul, gospel and house music


Congress Plaza Hotel

Enjoy unparalleled value and gracious hospitality at one of downtown Chicago’s most legendary hotels. Located across from Grant Park, with views of Lake Michigan, the Congress Plaza Hotel & Convention Center has been a Windy City icon since 1893, playing host to U.S. presidents, international celebrities and performers, foreign dignitaries and elite travelers from around the world.

Swissotel Chicago

Swissotel Chicago, designed by renowned Chicago architect Harry Weese, is an award winning, four-diamond, all-glass triangular luxury hotel. Swissotel Chicago offers 661 tastefully decorated and generously over-sized luxurious non-smoking rooms and suites, with a full range of exclusive amenities.

Hyatt Regency Chicago

Discover the difference and experience the sophistication at our downtown Chicago hotel. Set within the epicenter of the Windy City, our AAA Four Diamond Hyatt Regency Chicago is conveniently connected to the Illinois Center and located steps away from the Magnificent Mile. Explore popular attractions surrounding our Chicago hotel near Navy Pier and shop on Michigan Avenue.

Fairmont Millennium Park

Discover the Fairmont Chicago, Millennium Park, a beacon of modern design. The artful lobby of this downtown Chicago hotel near Grant Park beckons with a grand sense of arrival – at once, as fashionable as the city of Chicago – through crisp lines, dark woods and neutral finishes tinged with pops of color. Experience an exclusive level of luxury and service with Fairmont Gold – a privileged lifestyle hotel experience tucked away on the Fairmont’s 15th floor.

The Westin Michigan Avenue

Set on Chicago's famed Magnificent Mile, Westin Michigan Avenue offers guests a wide array of options to satisfy even the most avid shopper. There are countless restaurants, and numerous things to do in Chicago for families, couples, and friends alike to enjoy. Two such attractions are the midwest's most popular tourist and leisure destination, Navy Pier as well as The Hancock Tower.

The Godfrey Hotel

Reserve a private Igloo in I|O for a one of a kind private experience in Chicago. Keep warm while outside under the citylights enjoying cocktails and a few bites. As featured on WCIU's You & Me In The Morning, this is the perfect romantic venue to bring your date and sip champagne. Enjoy an 80 minute body-treatment massage featuring warm organic shea butter, hot towels and exfoliation. Spa Boutique is located on the 4th and open seven days a week.
